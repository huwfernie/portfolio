Yabee

Yabee; is Ebay spelled bakwards and with an extra 'e' added to help with pronouciation. It's the last project I did during my training at General Assembly and went from conception to this MVP in just over a week. The idea was to create a version of Ebay and Gumtree where it was also possible to list yourself, as a person willing to buy something. This would give sellers the option of a much quicker sale than waiting for an auction to end or for someone to respond to an add.

The Stack

The styling was taken from Gumtree.com, and at the time it was made many layout features would match to the pixel. The front end is Angular, while the back end is Ruby on rails talking to an SQL database.

I wanted potential sellers to be able to talk with potential buyers, so I also made a simple message service within the app which works well but took a disproportionate amount of time to impliment.
